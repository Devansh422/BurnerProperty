Our studio

We build homes, not projects.

Burner Properties started in 2012 with one obvious frustration: nobody wants to coordinate between a broker, an architect, a contractor and an interior designer. So we folded all four into one studio — same drawing, same team, same accountability, from the first plot visit to the last light fixture.

The story

Twelve years, one promise: show up.

We began in a two-room office in Bengaluru, turning a single farmhouse plot into a family home for a friend. Twelve years later, the rule is the same — the architect who drew your living room also walks the site the day the slab is poured.

Our crews are in-house, our suppliers long-term, our costing open-book. If something goes wrong, it lands on our desk, not yours.

What we live by

Three things we don't compromise on.

Every commission runs through the same three filters — if a decision breaks one, we don't take it.

01

One signature

The same studio owns every line of the contract. No sub-contract chains, no blame games, no "that's not our scope."

02

Open-book pricing

You see our material invoices at cost. We make our margin on design and delivery, not hidden mark-ups on steel or tile.

03

Date we said, date we mean

Handover dates are contractual, not aspirational. 98% of our homes hand over on time; for the 2%, we pay the rent.
