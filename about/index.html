The Official Playbook

Burner Properties strategic framework for real estate marketing, sales operations, and brand growth. Owner: Mr. Dinesh Kirola. Scope: Residential & Commercial. Primary region: Uttarakhand, India. Playbook release: March 2026.

Core Direction

Building trust. Delivering value.

From Rudrapur to Mukteshwar, we connect people with their dream properties through transparent processes and creative storytelling. Our operating model combines local intelligence, verified sourcing, and structured handover execution.

Every campaign and transaction follows the same field-tested workflow: source, verify, market, show, negotiate, close, and handover. This keeps communication clear across teams and predictable for every client.
